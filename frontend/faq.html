Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered.

What are your shipping policies for deliveries within India?

We offer standard and expedited shipping across India. Standard shipping for most items takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. Heavy or oversized equipment may have special shipping arrangements, which will be communicated at the time of order.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we have extensive experience with global logistics and can ship to most major ports worldwide. International shipping times and costs vary by destination. Please contact us for a specific quote.

What is the warranty on your equipment?

All our products come with a standard 2-year manufacturer's warranty against defects. Extended warranty options are available for select product lines. The warranty covers parts and labor for any manufacturing faults.

Can I request a custom-fabricated component?

Absolutely. Our engineering team specializes in custom solutions. Please get in touch via our contact page with your technical specifications and requirements, and we will work with you to design and fabricate the perfect component for your needs.

Do you provide GST invoicing for business purchases?

Yes, as an Indian company, we provide proper GST-compliant invoices for all B2B transactions. Please provide your company's GSTIN at the time of placing the order to ensure it is correctly reflected on the invoice.
