Our Story

Decades of Dedication to Maritime Excellence

Forged in the Heart of Mumbai

Founded in 1998, Ayush Enterprises began its journey in a small workshop in Mumbai, driven by a singular passion: to provide the Indian maritime industry with components that were not only reliable but world-class. From our early days serving local fishing fleets to now equipping international cargo vessels and defense submarines, our commitment to quality has never wavered. Our history is deeply rooted in this vibrant port city, and we are proud to contribute to its legacy as a critical hub for global trade.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted partner for marine operators by engineering and supplying technologically advanced, durable, and mission-critical equipment. We aim to enhance safety, efficiency, and performance for every vessel we serve, ensuring that our clients can navigate the world's oceans with absolute confidence.
