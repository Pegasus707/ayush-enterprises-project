Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your products in a timely and secure manner. Standard shipping within India typically takes 5-7 business days. For oversized or heavy industrial equipment, specialized freight arrangements will be made, and delivery times may vary. All shipping charges will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Returns & Exchange Policy

We accept returns for defective or damaged products within 14 days of receipt. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact our support team via the contact page with your order number and details about the product defect. We will provide you with instructions on how to proceed with the return or exchange.
